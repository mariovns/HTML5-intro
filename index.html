My Favourite Recipes

This is the first recipe. Follow these steps:

  1. Lit the stove.
  2. Put a vessel containing half cup water and half cup milk.
  3. Put 1 teaspoon tea-leaves in the vessel and 2 teaspoon sugar.
  4. Let it boil and then add ginger.
  5. After 5 minutes, serve it in the cup along with Namkeen!
